Why "Raise"?

Social Media has ingrained into the string of our daily activities close in vigour to simply existing. For our LGBTQ+ community, it became a million opportunities, but with it came a slew of reasons to oppose. The lack of a safe, no-hate closed community to discuss, reason and question in a way that only they can resonate with is the only lacking point to aid to the betterment of their lives.

A safe, no-hate community platform to virtually provide to the LGBTQ+'s mental and physical needs. A health assistant, mental health discussion forum, group and personal chat --for finding the right people to talk about one's needs and thoughts-- and finding the right health care providers would be the preliminary features of our solution.
